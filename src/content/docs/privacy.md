The App Fair Project web site at appfair.org does not collect any personal information, except that which has been explicitly submitted through donation or contact forms.

Our website does not use cookies or any other tracking technologies to monitor or analyze your usage of our site.

Web server logging maintained by our Content Distribution Network (CDN) may contain information about the source IP address and web browser agent.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
